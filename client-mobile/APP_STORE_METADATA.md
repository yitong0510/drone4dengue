**💡 Personalized Recommendations**
• Get preventive measures tailored to your risk level
• Action plans for High, Medium, and Low risk scenarios
• Evidence-based recommendations with source links

**📊 Organization Dashboard**
• Monitor multiple locations simultaneously

• View comprehensive risk analytics
• Perfect for schools, offices, and community centers

**📈 Dengue Case Tracking**
• View real-time dengue case data in your region

**🔍 Detailed Risk Analysis**
• Comprehensive risk breakdown with multiple factors

Dengue fever affects millions of people worldwide, with cases increasing in tropical and subtropical regions. Early detection and prevention are crucial to protecting yourself and your loved ones.
